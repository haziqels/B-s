School overview

At Little Playhouse, we strive to give every child the best profound head start in the area of formal education in our play school. Our role is focused on developing a mindset characterized by curiosity and the quest for knowledge, hence reducing the chances of any child dropping out of school at the higher educational level. We seek to equip every child with the educational resilience to pursue different career paths to any level they desire. We provide full-day and half-day infant daycare, toddlers play school & preschool, and after-school transit programs. Feel free to contact us to know more!
